About

Chiebuka Christopher, a Commonwealth scholar at Imperial College London is a lecturer in the Mechatronics Engineering programme at Bowen University, Nigeria. He was an assistant lecturer at Landmark University, Nigeria. His current research focuses on tactile sensors, and material informatics in additive manufacturing.

He holds a distinction in Master of Science (MSc.) in Mechatronics Engineering that's jointly awarded by the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) and Pan African University, Institute for Basic Sciences, Technology and Innovation (PAUSTI), Kenya. His MSc. research focused on developing a tactile sensor for fruit ripeness detection. He also holds first-class honours in Bachelor of Engineering degree in Mechanical Engineering at Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Nigeria.

Chiebuka has lectured various Mechanical Engineering and Mechatronics Engineering courses at both Landmark University and Bowen University, Nigeria, and has successfully supervised several undergraduate projects. Chiebuka has reviewed for reputable journals, including the IEEE Sensors Journal and the International Journal on Interactive Design and Manufacturing (IJIDeM).

He has published over twenty (20) research papers in both local and international journals of repute. He has received many awards and scholarships, including the best lecturer award by the Bowen University Engineering Students Association (2022), Commonwealth shared scholarships (2022), The Government of Ireland-Higher Education scholarship (GOI-HES) (2022), The RSIF-ICIPE scholarship (2022), Graduate Assistantships (2022). He also received the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) research grant (2019), Best student paper award, USA (2019), Best MSc. thesis award (2019), African Union scholarship (2017), and College of Engineering overall best-graduating student (2015).

Chiebuka is a COREN registered Engineer, a member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE), and the International Association of Engineers (IAENG). He is a highly motivated hardworking professional with an affinity for harnessing information that drives excellence.
